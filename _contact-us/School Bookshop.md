<h3>School Bookshop</h3>
<p>Appointed school bookshop vendor: <strong>D Y Education Service Trading</strong> (w.e.f.
1 Jan 2024).</p>
<p>Operating hours</p>
<p>Mondays - Fridays</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>11:00 am to 12:30 pm</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>2:00 pm to 3:30 pm</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Parents/Guardians</strong> are only allowed to come into the school
premises to make purchases at the bookshop during the operating hours.</p>
<p>Alternatively, your child will be able to make purchases during their
respective recess times from 9:00 am to 10:35 am.</p>
