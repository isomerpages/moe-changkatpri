<p><strong>Payment of PSLE Examination Fees via Candidates Portal (CP)</strong>
</p>
<p>Permanent Residents and International students who are currently paying
school fees via GIRO would have their PSLE examination fees deducted on
19 May 2026.</p>
<p>PSLE examination fees for non-Singaporean school candidates are as follows.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Permanent residents S$105</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>International Students S$385</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>School will no longer be collecting cash payments for PSLE examination
fees. Cash-paying candidates or whose GIRO deductions for PSLE exam fees
were unsuccessful, are to make payment through Candidates Portal (CP) (
<a href="https://myexams.seab.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://myexams.seab.gov.sg</a>) from 26 May to 8 June 2026.</p>
<p>If you need further clarifications, please contact the General Office
at 6783 0923.</p>
